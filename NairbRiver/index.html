Nairobi River Water Quality Monitoring: A practical Google Cloud Platform Innovative solution to the design and implementation of a real time pollution monitoring for decision support.

River water quality and environmental pressures

Why We Care

  • Healthy rivers support many species and habitats
  • Clean waters support agricultural and industrial activities
  • Rivers are primary sources of clean water for domestic use
  • Rivers support recreational activities and tourism

What We Are Monitoring

  • Physical and chemical water quality parameters (Chlorophil, TSS, Turbidity)
  • Biological parameters (Aquatic plants, Algae, Fish and Invertebrates)
  • Hydrological Parameters - Elevation, Water level and Flow

Our Objectives

  • Evaluation of the pollution status of rivers
  • Monitor long-term changes in river water quality
  • Provide scientific basis for planning water pollution control strategies
  • Assess compliance with the Water Quality Objectives (WQOs)
  • Compile Water Quality Index (WQI) to reflect the overall state and trend of the health of rivers

Why GEE Cloud Platform

  • Server Integrations - Inbuilt IDE and development tools, simplicity for automatic deployment
  • Ingenuity - no worry about underlying services and their configurations
  • First-rate Security Level – experience top most data security
  • High Availability - Globally scalable, fully managed infrastructure and access to multiterabyte data catalog
  • Improved Computation power - GEE provides ultrafast, geospatial processing and computation power
  • Improved optical performance – Extremely high resolution satellite data, with high update frequency for continuous improvement
